LOCATION

San Antonio Marriott Rivercenter

101 Bowie St, San Antonio, TX

This year, we're bringing RubyConf to the San Antonio Marriott Rivercenter, overlooking the famous San Antonio Riverwalk. As such, we'll be just steps away from the vibrant shops and restaurants that line the banks of the San Antonio River.

HOTELS

We have a dedicated hotel room block for RubyConf attendees at the Marriott Rivercenter! To make sure that you're getting our negotiated RubyConf attendee rate of $219/night, please use this link: to register, or call 1-877-491-5138 and ask for the RubyConf 2015 block.

(All reservations must be booked by Friday, October 23, 2015 to receive the group rate.)

GETTING THERE

San Antonio International Airport (SAT) services many US and some international cities. The airport is not far from our venue -- approximately a 20-minute taxi ride (approximately $25-30).

For those looking to take San Antonio's VIA public transit system, the #5 bus line picks up from the far west end of Terminal B. The #5 bus costs $1.20 one-way, and takes approximately 30 min to get downtown. If you disembark at the St. Mary's/Commerce stop, it is approximately a .5 mile walk to the conference venue.
